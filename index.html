What we do

Noise Riot is a band of brothers, forged in steel from the ancient planets of Kruyupinopendøg and Viratriå, respectively. We use code to help festival goers, and improve the experience of life through computers.

Powered by MapQuest

By utilizing MapQuest's powerful API, we leverage real-time data and traffic statistics to analyze your position in relation to any and all disruptions nearby.

Protect Your Wallet

Use our software to know exactly when to call that Uber or Lyft and save money by avoiding surge pricing and next morning credit card surprises.

Always Improving

Our software has plans for improvement, and built in just 18 hours, shows great promise for future algorithmic learnings. The next version will include MapQuest's route planner, so you'll know exactly the route to tell your driver when the time to GO arrives. Many other improvements and direct integrations are on the horizon.

Your Time is of the Most Value

We designed Chill or Go (CoG) to be simple, and provide quick information so you can increase the time in your life meant for fun.
